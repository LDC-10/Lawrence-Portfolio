Projects

  • Automatic Goldfish Feeder
    with Water Changer Mechanism

    The automatic goldfish feeder system with a water filter and changer mechanism, powered by an Arduino Uno microcontroller, turbidity sensor, submersible pump, and servo motor, offers a convenient and efficient solution for feeding goldfish and maintaining optimal water quality in an aquarium. By automating the feeding process and monitoring water conditions, this system ensures the health and well-being of goldfish while reducing the need for manual intervention.
